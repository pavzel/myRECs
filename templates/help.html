Help Page

What is myRECs?

This site was created to store and share RECords about places which you have visited, or want to visit, or may want to visit (places RECommended by the other users).

It is primarily for travelers who use cars but everybody else is welcome too.

Information is place-centered. A place can have just one interesting site (like Mont-Saint-Michel) or hundreds of them (like Paris) but if these sites can be reached on foot or by car within 5-20 minutes, all of them are considered to be located in the same place.

What information about a place is stored?

For each place you can add and edit whenever you want the following information:

  • Place name (required)
  • Country (required)
  • Your opinion about the place (by default 0)
  • Whether you've visited the place (by default "No")
  • A link to a website about the place (optional)
  • A link to a photo of the place (optional)
  • Your comments about the place (optional)

Your opinion may be positive but it may as well be negative. Thus the opinion scale looks like this:

  • -3 means "never go there"
  • -2 means "uninteresting and unfriendly"
  • -1 means "boring"
  •  0 means "rather nice but nothing special"
  • +1 means "interesting or especially nice"
  • +2 means "very interesting"
  • +3 means "outstanding/unique"

Thus the other users may get your RECommendation to visit or not to visit a place.

You can also delete a place that you added. However, it is irreversible! Please, think twice before doing this.

Important! To be able to add, edit, or delete place information you have to log in. If you have no username yet, please, sign up. No personal information is required. The site is supposed to be as anonymous as it's possible. At the moment, you don't need to provide even your email address.

The other users can also add new places. Or they can add their own information about any already added (by any user) place except for the place name and country.

Information added by all users is visible to everybody. However, it is anonymous.

How places are displayed?

I. All places (Home Page)

On the home page you can see all places added by all users to the database. They are sorted by the "Users' opinion" (averaged opinion of all users who rated this place).

At the moment, not more than 15 places are displayed on a page. However, with "First", "Previous", "Next", "Last" buttons on the bottom of the page, you can get to all other places.

II. Selected places

You can limit the displayed set of places by pressing Select in the menu. There you can select one or several countries for which places exist in the database.

III. RECommended places

As different people have different tastes, the top places according to "Users' opinion" may not be very interesting personally to you. That is why, by pressing RECommended in the menu, you can get personal RECommendations ("REC-opinion") based on similarity of your tastes with the other users. A similar approach is used for movie and book recommendation sites.

Important! To get RECommendations you have to log in. If you have no username yet, please, sign up. Also you have to provide your opinion about at least one of the places that were rated by other users. Otherwise either (i) your tastes are unknown, or (ii) your and other users' tastes are incomparable.
